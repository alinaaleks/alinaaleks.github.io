### Question(s)

- Are our prices reasonable?
- Why I don't have cash when I need to pay to supplier, even though overall I make profit?
- What are our most profitable products?

### Problem / situation

A chocolate workshop needed to understand:

- the profitability of its products and the accuracy of its prices
- how to set up cash flows to eliminate cash gaps.

### Goal / solution

Analyze income and expenses and develop a pricing model.

### Result / insights

- Profitable and unprofitable items identified.
- Prices adjusted.
- Financial planning improved.

Google Sheets, Apps Script
